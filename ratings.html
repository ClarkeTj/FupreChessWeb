📖 Achievements Glossary

  • 🏆 FCC Champion – Winner of the annual Fupre Chess Club Championship.
  • 🏅 FCM Winner – Winner of the annual Fupre Chess Masters.
  • 🥇 FCL Winner – Winner of the annual Fupre Chess League.
  • ⏱️ Grand Rapid Winner – Champion of the montly Rapid Tournament.
  • ⚡ Lightning Blitz Winner – First place in the weekly Blitz Tournament.
  • 🌟 Rising Star – Award for newcomers and intermediate players showing strong performance.

These achievements celebrate dedication, skill, and growth — keeping our chess community competitive and inspiring.
