Qualification Paths

Rating Spot (1)

Must play ≥ 40 rated rapid games (vs players ≥ 1500) and at least one Grand Rapid. If ineligible or declines, the spot passes to the next eligible player.

Fupre Chess Tour (3)

Top 3 on the semester Tour leaderboard qualify directly.

Grand Swiss (2)

Top 2 finishers of the Grand Swiss qualify.

Classic TJ (2)

Top 2 finishers of the open 7-round Round Robin qualify.

If a player qualifies via multiple paths, their extra slot passes to the next eligible player from that path.

Qualification System for FUPRE Chess Masters (FCM)

The FUPRE Chess Masters will feature 8 players, selected through 4 qualification paths.

1 Rating Spot 1 Player

Awarded to the highest-rated player in FUPRE Rapid ratings.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Must have participated in at least one Grand Rapid event during the qualification period.
  • Must have played a minimum of 40 rated rapid games.
  • Of those games, the majority must be against opponents rated 1500 Elo or higher.
  • If the top-rated player is ineligible or declines, the spot passes to the next highest-rated eligible player.

2 FUPRE Chess Tour 3 Players

Timeline: Runs each semester from the opening week to one week before FCM.

Scoring System:

  • Lightning Blitz 1st: 5pts • 2nd: 4pts • 3rd: 3pts • 4th: 2pts • 5th–10th: 1pt
  • Grand Rapid 1st: 15pts • 2nd: 12pts • 3rd: 10pts • 4th: 8pts • 5th: 6pts • 6th: 5pts • 7th: 4pts • 8th: 3pts • 9th: 2pts • 10th: 1pt
  • Other Major FUPRE Events 1st: 25pts • 2nd: 20pts • 3rd: 15pts • 4th: 12pts • 5th: 10pts • 6th: 8pts • 7th: 6pts • 8th: 4pts • 9th: 2pts • 10th: 1pt

Qualification: The Top 3 players on the Tour leaderboard earn spots in FCM.

3 Grand Swiss 2 Players

Format: Open Swiss-system tournament (7–9 rounds, depending on number of participants).

Qualification: The Top 2 finishers qualify for FCM.

4Classic TJ2 Players

Format: Open 7-Round Round Robin Tournament.

Qualification: The Top 2 finishers qualify for FCM.

🔄 Overlap Clarification

A player cannot qualify via multiple paths.

If a player already holds a qualification spot (e.g., via Rating Spot) and places in the Tour, Grand Swiss, or ClarkeTJ Classic, the spot passes to the next eligible player in that event/standings. This ensures 8 unique participants for FCM.
