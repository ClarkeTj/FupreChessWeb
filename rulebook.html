Club Rulebook

Review the Fupre Chess Club official rules and conduct guidelines below.

  1. Eligibility: All registered students of FUPRE are eligible to compete.
  2. Time Control: Standard games will follow a 15+10 rapid format unless stated otherwise.
  3. Fair Play: Cheating (e.g. engine assistance, multiple accounts) will result in a ban.
  4. Scoring: Win = 1 pt, Draw = 0.5 pt, Loss = 0 pt.
  5. Rating: Player ratings are updated after every approved match.
  6. Behavior: Respect for opponents and arbiters is mandatory. Unsportsmanlike conduct is penalized.
  7. Disputes: All disputes must be submitted in writing within 24 hours of the match.
